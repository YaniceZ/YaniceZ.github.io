Biography

Hello! I am Yanice Zeng, currently a Master's student in Geology at the University of British Columbia (UBC) in Vancouver, Canada.

My research focuses on sedimentary processes, mineral formations, and the geochemical cycles that shape our planet's crust. Through fieldwork, laboratory experiments, and computational modeling, I aim to understand how geological materials and environments evolve over time.

On this website, you can find updates on my latest research findings, access my publications, and learn about ongoing projects. I also share occasional blog posts discussing my academic journey, travel experiences during field studies, and insights into the geoscience community.

If you have any questions or would like to connect, please feel free to contact me.
